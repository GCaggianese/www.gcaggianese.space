When 見 follows a perceptual verb means that the action is in fact
percieved.

# Structures

\<perceptual verb\> + 見

-   昨天我在夜市看見你。

## Negation

我的手機在那裡？ 我沒看見啊！

## Questions

老師說的，你是不是聽見了？

## Usage

There's a difference between using and not using 見:

-   我沒聽她說話。
    -   Here the idea is she didn't listen at all (ignored).
-   我沒聽見她說話。
    -   Here she couln't listen, sound never successfully reached (no
        successful perception).

3.  是誰在唱歌？你 <u>看見他了</u> 嗎？
4.  我的書，你 <u>看見了</u> 嗎？
